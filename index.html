    From the Founder
    Words from
    Zubair

    It all started in 2010, when I embarked on my investing and trading journey in the Stock Market. But in 2013, my world shifted as I stumbled upon Bitcoin. From that moment, nothing in the financial world has captivated me more.

    Initially, I experimented with Bitcoin, unaware of its true potential. But as I delved deeper, I unearthed truths about money and finance far more profound than anything I learned in my MBA classrooms.

    This newfound knowledge spurred a four-year transformation, morphing me from a stock investor to a staunch Bitcoin maximalist. From 2017, I not only invest aggressively in Bitcoin — I dedicate myself to helping others understand the complexities of Bitcoin investing, empowering them to secure their financial future.

    We stand at the precipice of a colossal monetary reset. Governments are overspending and printing exorbitant amounts of money, devaluing their currencies. This is where Bitcoin shines — a decentralized monetary network, free from manipulation, accessible to all.

    This realization fueled the creation of CoinSats — a platform to help people become future millionaires by adopting the future of money before everyone else does.

    — Zubair Abdul Jabbar
    Founder, CoinSats
