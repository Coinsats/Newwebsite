From the Founder
Zubair's Story

It all started in 2010 when I began my investing journey in the Stock Market. But in 2013, everything changed when I discovered Bitcoin. From that moment, nothing in finance has captivated me more.

As I delved deeper, I unearthed truths about money far more profound than anything I learned in my MBA classrooms. This led me from stock investor to staunch Bitcoin maximalist.

From 2017, I've invested aggressively in Bitcoin and dedicated myself to helping others understand it — empowering them to secure their financial future before the inevitable global monetary reset.

Governments are overspending and printing money, devaluing currencies. Bitcoin is the answer: decentralized, manipulation-free, accessible to all. That's why I created CoinSats.

— Zubair Abdul Jabbar
Founder, CoinSats
